About us

Welcome to Uber your ultimate ride to anywhere, anytime!

From your daily commutes to spontaneous adventures, we are here to fuel your journey with speed, safety, and style. One tap, endless possibilities. Ready to roll ?

Uber began in 2009 with a simple but powerful idea: making transportation as easy and reliable as tapping a button. Founded in San Francisco by Travis Kalanick and Garrett Camp, Uber launched in 2010 with a black car service that let riders request a ride via an app. It was a game-changer, offering convenience and dependability in a way traditional taxis could not.What started as a local solution quickly grew into a global phenomenon. Uber expanded to offer a variety of ride options, from affordable everyday rides with UberX to shared rides with UberPOOL.
